Levels and Badging

Earn badges or certificate by contributing to Bolo India. Speak a sentence in language of your choice or validate the sentence that is spoken on your desired language. Share badges in your social circle, build the hype, motivate your friends and family to contribute towards the cause. The strength of Bolo India is in the diversity of the people i.e. YOU.
